# Visant.AI Web Design Guidelines

These guidelines describe the warm, minimal, Claude.ai-inspired design system for the Visant.AI marketing site.

---

## 1. Brand Principles

- **Warm & approachable:** Use earthy, warm tones that feel friendly and inviting
- **Clean & minimal:** Prioritize clarity and whitespace over visual complexity
- **Human-centric:** Design should feel personal and accessible, not corporate or sterile
- **Clear hierarchy:** Use typography and spacing to create obvious information hierarchy

---

## 2. Color System

### Core Colors

Primary: #CC785C (copper/orange accent)
Text Dark: #1A1A1A (headings)
Text Body: #2D2D2D (body copy)
Text Muted: #6B6B6B (secondary)
BG Cream: #F5F3EE (page background)
BG Warm: #EDE8E0 (sections, footer)
BG White: #FFFFFF (cards)
Border: #E5E0D8 (subtle borders)

### Usage: Keep warm, light backgrounds. Use copper sparingly for accents only. Generous whitespace. Subtle shadows.

---

## 3. Typography

Font: Inter (weights 400, 500, 600, 700)

H1 Hero: 2.5rem, weight 600, tight spacing
H2 Section: 2rem, weight 600
Body: 1rem, weight 400, line-height 1.6-1.7

---

## 4. Components

Buttons: 8px radius, subtle shadow, lift on hover
Cards: White bg, 1px border, 12px radius, 2rem padding
Hover: translateY(-2px) + enhanced shadow

All transitions: 0.15s ease

---

This warm, minimal design prioritizes clarity and human connection.
