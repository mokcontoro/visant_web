How It Works

1

Point a Device

Prop up that old phone, tablet, or laptop in the corner of the room. Open Visant in the browser — that's the camera. Nothing to buy.

2

Describe what you want to know about, just like you'd tell a pet sitter. Visant's AI checks the camera against your rule while you're away.

When your rule matches, an email lands in your inbox with the photo of the moment — so you see exactly what happened, when it happened.
