How It Works

1

Point a Camera

Use the plug-and-play Visant Camera ($199), or turn any phone, tablet, or laptop into a free web camera in your browser.

2

Type Your Safety Rule

Describe the violation in plain English. Visant captures images on your schedule and two AI models check each one against your rule.

3

Get Email Alerts

An alert is sent only when both AI models agree a rule was broken — dual-AI consensus designed to keep false alarms low.
