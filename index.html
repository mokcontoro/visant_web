Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical skills?

No. If you can type a description like "Alert if the door is left open", you can use Visant. The AI handles the rest.

Can I use my own camera?

Yes! You can use our Web Camera feature on any device with a browser (laptop, phone, tablet) to test the system without buying hardware.

How accurate is the AI?

We use a dual-agent consensus system (OpenAI GPT + Google Gemini) to verify every detection, resulting in less than 1% false alarms.

Does the hardware include AI monitoring?

No. Hardware and AI licenses are sold separately. After adding your camera, purchase an AI license on the Billing page to enable monitoring.

Can I move a license between cameras?

Yes! You can remove a license from one camera and assign it to another anytime on the Billing page. Licenses are flexible and not locked to a specific device.
